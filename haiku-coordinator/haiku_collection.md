# Haiku Collection: Five Themes, Five Languages

## Theme 1: Morning Dew

### English
Drops on grass blades gleam,
Nature's tiny jewels shine bright,
New day softly dawns.

*Cultural elements: Natural imagery, contemplative tone, focus on renewal*

### Japanese
朝露光る (Asa tsuyu hikaru)
草葉に玉の (Kusaba ni tama no)
夜明けの音 (Yoake no ne)

*Transliteration: Asa tsuyu hikaru / Kusaba ni tama no / Yoake no ne*
*Cultural elements: Traditional kigo (asa tsuyu - morning dew), wabi-sabi aesthetic, focus on subtle sounds*

### Spanish
Rocío matinal,
Perlas en campos dorados,
Sol besa la tierra.

*Cultural elements: Mediterranean agricultural imagery, passionate but measured language, connection to land*

### French
Rosée du matin,
Gouttes de cristal sur l'herbe,
L'aube se révèle.

*Cultural elements: Romantic refined aesthetic, elegant language, garden imagery, focus on revelation*

### Arabic
ندى الصباح (Nada al-sabah)
جواهر على الزهور (Jawahir 'ala al-zuhur)
فجر جديد يتجلى (Fajr jadid yatajalla)

*Transliteration: Nada al-sabah / Jawahir 'ala al-zuhur / Fajr jadid yatajalla*
*Cultural elements: Classical poetic imagery, desert garden context, respectful language, divine revelation*
