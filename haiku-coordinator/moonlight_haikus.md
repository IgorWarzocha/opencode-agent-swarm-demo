# Moonlight on Water - Multilingual Haiku Collection

## Japanese (日本語)
秋の月影 (aki no tsuki kage) - 5 syllables
水面に静かに映る (minamo ni shizuka ni utsuru) - 7 syllables  
川のせせらぎ音 (kawa no seseragi oto) - 5 syllables

*Syllable count: 5-7-5 (traditional Japanese morae structure)*
*Incorporates "tsuki" (月), "kage" (shadow/reflection), and "kawa" (river) with traditional autumn moon imagery and nature poetry aesthetics.*

## Mandarin Chinese (中文)
月影落江水 (yuè yǐng luò jiāng shuǐ) - 5 syllables
波光映照天地心 (bō guāng yìng zhào tiān dì xīn) - 7 syllables

*Syllable count: 5-7-5*
